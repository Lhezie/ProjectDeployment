About Me

My journey into the world of technology began with a passion for creativity and problem-solving, which naturally led me to pursue a career as a front-end developer. Transitioning into tech has been an exhilarating experience, allowing me to combine my love for design with the technical skills needed to bring ideas to life. However, I’ve realized that my ultimate goal is to become a full-stack web developer, where I can contribute to both the front-end and back-end aspects of a project. This ambition has driven me to join AltSchool Africa, where I am now committed to deepening my knowledge in back-end development. With a dedication to learning and a strong work ethic, I’m excited to take on this new challenge and move closer to achieving my full potential in the tech industry.


Beyond the lines of code, I find solace and expression in writing spoken word poetry. It's a creative outlet that allows me to explore the depths of language and emotion, much like how coding allows me to craft intricate solutions to complex problems. My love for learning, whether it’s mastering a new programming language or perfecting a verse in poetry, keeps me motivated and constantly evolving. I believe that the combination of these passions—technology and poetry—makes me a well-rounded individual, ready to tackle any challenge and create meaningful, impactful work in all areas of my life.

Reasons why AltSchool Africa is a compelling choice for me

Practical, Skill-Based Learning

AltSchool Africa focuses on hands-on, practical training, ensuring that students gain the skills directly applicable in the tech industry, rather than just theoretical knowledge.

Affordable and Accessible Education

The program is designed to be financially accessible, offering high-quality education at a fraction of the cost of traditional universities, making tech education attainable for more people.

Industry-Relevant Curriculum

The curriculum is constantly updated to reflect current industry trends and technologies, ensuring that students are learning the most in-demand skills needed in today’s job market.

Strong Community and Support System

Students at AltSchool are part of a vibrant community that offers peer support, mentoring, and networking opportunities, helping them grow both personally and professionally.

Focus on Employability

AltSchool emphasizes preparing students for the job market, with career services, portfolio development, and job placement assistance to help graduates secure employment.

Flexible Learning Environment

With options for remote learning, AltSchool provides flexibility for students to learn at their own pace and balance their education with other commitments.
